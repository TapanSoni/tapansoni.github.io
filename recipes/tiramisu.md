# Tiramisu

Ingredients
===========
1. 16 oz Mascarpone cheese - 450 grams
2. 4 egg yolks
3. 2/3 cup of granulated sugar - 133 grams
4. 1 tsp vanilla extract
5. 1/4 tsp salt
6. 1.5 cups of cold heavy cream - 360 grams
7. Lady fingers - 30 to 60
8. Strong black coffee - espresso preferred - about 1.5 cups cooled down to room temp
9. Cocoa powder - for dusting

Method
======
1. Mascarpone cheese -> Whip on medium for 30 to 60 secs
2. In a bowl - egg yolks + sugar -> put over a LOW double boiler - whip on medium/high for 2 mins - DO NOT overmix
3. Whipped mascarpone cheese + egg yolks & sugar mixture + salt + vanilla -> mix/whip until JUST COMBINED - DO NOT overmix
4. Separate bowl - whip heavy cream until soft peaks
5. Fold in the mascarpone cheese/egg yolk/sugar/salt/vanilla mixture into the heavy cream soft peaks mixture - gently - DO NOT overmix
6. Assembly - dip lady fingers into the coffee - 1 sec per side - quickly!
7. Assembly - line the bottom of a square baking dish with dipped lady fingers > then put over about half of the cream mixture > dipped lady fingers > rest of the cream mixture
8. Smooth the cream out > chill overnight
9. Sift cocoa powder evenly over the top of the tiramisu before serving
10. Cut into squares and enjoy!
